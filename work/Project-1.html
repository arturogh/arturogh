This project explores a desktop site by REI, separate from REI.com, that helps find camping trip destinations, as well as planning and buying gear for them.

The premise is to help people “enjoy the great outdoors, purchase more products through REI, and become more involved in environmental stewardship” by building a Website that pushes (more) people to go on more camping trips together.

Project Brief

As detailed in the project brief, this site should enable users to:

  • Find and book camping destinations.
  • Discover and book other outdoor activities to take part in while camping.
  • Plan the trip with a trip checklist.
  • Receive intelligent suggestions for the checklist based on campers destination and activities.
  • Purchase products available already on REI.com directly from this new website.

Research

The domain research focused on a paper on American camping, which provided important insights, such as that camping is a group activity done with friends or/and family, and that activities are an essential part of it.

After this, a comparative of sites with similar objectives to the ones this site has would later inform the fields, information and functionality each page should have.

With this data, I interviewed 5 people between the ages of 18 and 54. The key takeaways were “it’s really hard to find places to go camping to” and “It’s hard planning group camping trip”.
