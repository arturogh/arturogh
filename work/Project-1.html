This concept website by REI is a response to the diminishing number of people going on camping trips: campers are having a hard time planning trips and finding where to go. This site aims to solve that by providing a guided experience finding destinations and a platoform to coordinate trip details as a group. The main goal is to maintain REI's leadership as a brand, a store and outdoor travel facilitator.
